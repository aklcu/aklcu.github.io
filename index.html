What is ultimate?

It’s an exciting, non-contact team sport, combining the passing skills of Netball and the agility of Soccer, all while throwing a disc! The aim of the game is to complete a pass in the opponent’s end-zone to score a point. A player cannot run while in possession of the disc, however, can pivot from one foot like a netball player to make a pass to a teammate. Fast-paced and competitive or easy going and social, ultimate is a great sport for people of all levels of fitness to enjoy.
